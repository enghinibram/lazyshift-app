How is net salary calculated in 2026?

Three mandatory contributions are deducted from gross salary (based on Romanian tax law):

  • Pension (CAS) — 25% of gross salary
  • Health insurance (CASS) — 10% of gross salary
  • Income tax — 10% of taxable income (gross minus CAS, CASS and personal deduction)

Bonuses (night shift, overtime) are added to gross before the calculation.

What is the personal deduction?

Employees earning a gross salary up to 3,600 RON benefit from a personal deduction that reduces the taxable base. The exact amount depends on the number of dependants and the gross salary.
