Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Who we are

This site, lazyshift.app, is operated by Ibram Enghin, an individual based in Romania. Contact: contact@lazyshift.app

2. What data we collect

The site collects data in two ways:

  • Anonymous technical data — via Google Analytics 4 (GA4), if you accept cookies: pages visited, session duration, device type, country. This data is anonymised and does not identify you personally.
  • Account data (optional) — if you create an account via Supabase: your email address and saved shift settings. Passwords are stored encrypted. We do not store financial data.

3. Why we collect this data

  • GA4 — to understand how the app is used and improve features (legitimate interest / consent).
  • Supabase account — to save your shift pattern between sessions (performance of a contract/service).

4. Cookies

We use analytics cookies (Google Analytics) only if you accept via the banner on the site. You can withdraw consent at any time by clearing your browser data (Settings → Clear site data) or declining on your next visit.

We do not use advertising or third-party tracking cookies for any other purpose.

5. How long we keep data

  • GA4 data — 14 months (Google Analytics default setting).
  • Supabase account data — for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion at any time.

6. Who we share data with

We do not sell or share your data with third parties, except the service providers we use:

  • Google Analytics — traffic analysis (Google privacy policy)
  • Supabase — account storage (Supabase privacy policy)
  • GitHub Pages / Cloudflare — hosting and CDN

7. Your rights (GDPR)

Under EU Regulation 2016/679, you have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Rectify incorrect data
  • Erase your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Object to processing
  • Data portability

For any request, write to: contact@lazyshift.app

8. Changes

This policy may be updated. The date of the last modification is shown at the top of this page.
